Research

The University of Ghana Computer Science Department is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation in the field of computer science. Our faculty members are actively engaged in a wide range of research projects, and we are proud to have a strong record of success in securing funding for our research endeavors.




Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Data Science and Machine Learning
  • Graphics and Visualization
  • High-Performance Computing
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Software Engineering
  • Theoretical Computer Science



Research Facilities

The department has a number of well-equipped research facilities, including:

  • A high-performance computing cluster
  • A number of specialized research labs
  • A dedicated research library



Research Funding

The department has a strong track record of securing funding for its research endeavors. We have received funding from a variety of sources, including:

  • Government agencies
  • Private foundations
  • Industry sponsors



Research Opportunities

The department offers a number of research opportunities for students and postdocs. We encourage students to explore our research areas and to contact faculty members who are working in areas of interest.

For more information about our research programs, please visit our website or contact us at compsci@ug.edu.gh.
