Students

Student Life.

The University of Ghana Computer Science Department is committed to providing a vibrant and supportive student life for our students. We offer a wide range of opportunities for students to get involved in extracurricular activities, connect with fellow students, and develop their leadership skills.

  • Student organizations: We have over 20 student organizations, including the Computer Science Club, the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), and the Women in Computer Science (WiCS) club.
  • Internships and research opportunities: We offer a variety of internships and research opportunities for students to gain hands-on experience in the field of computer science.
  • Career services: Our career services office provides students with resources and support to help them find their dream jobs after graduation.




Academic Support.

The University of Ghana Computer Science Department is committed to providing our students with the academic support they need to succeed in their studies. We offer a variety of resources and services to help students achieve their academic goals.

Academic advising: Our academic advisors are available to meet with students to discuss their academic progress, course selection, and career goals.

Tutoring services: We offer tutoring services in a variety of computer science courses.

Supplemental instruction (SI) sessions: SI sessions are led by peer tutors who help students in difficult courses.



Student Success.

Our students are our greatest asset. We are proud of the many accomplishments of our alumni, who have gone on to become successful leaders in the field of computer science.
