About the Department of Computer Science

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana, a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment where students are empowered to become the next generation of innovators and leaders in the ever-evolving field of computing.



Our Mission

The Department of Computer Science is committed to providing world-class education, research, and service in the field of computing, fostering a diverse and inclusive community of scholars, and contributing to the advancement of knowledge and technological innovation in Ghana and beyond.



Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer science, renowned for its rigorous academic programs, groundbreaking research, and transformative impact on society.



Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service, consistently seeking to push the boundaries of knowledge and innovation.

  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and ingenuity, encouraging our students and faculty to explore new ideas and develop cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems.

  • Collaboration: We foster a spirit of teamwork and collaboration, recognizing that the power of computing lies in its ability to connect and empower individuals.

  • Diversity and Inclusion: We value and celebrate diversity, creating an inclusive environment where all students and faculty feel welcomed, respected, and supported.

  • Social Responsibility: We recognize our responsibility to apply our knowledge and expertise to address societal challenges and contribute to the betterment of Ghana and the world.


Our Programs

The Department of Computer Science offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs, designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in the ever-changing computing landscape. Our programs emphasize theoretical foundations, practical applications, and the development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills.



Our Faculty

Our faculty comprises a team of highly accomplished researchers and educators, dedicated to providing students with an exceptional learning experience. Our faculty members are actively engaged in cutting-edge research, ensuring that our curriculum remains at the forefront of the field.



Our Students

Our students are a diverse and talented group, representing a wide range of backgrounds and interests. They are passionate about computing and eager to make a positive impact on the world.



Our Impact

Our graduates are making significant contributions to the computing industry, both in Ghana and around the world. They are employed by leading technology companies, government agencies, and academic institutions, and they are leading the way in the development of innovative solutions to real-world problems.



Join Us

We invite you to join our vibrant and intellectually stimulating community of scholars and embark on a journey of discovery and innovation in the exciting field of computer science. Together, we can shape the future of computing and make a lasting impact on the world.
